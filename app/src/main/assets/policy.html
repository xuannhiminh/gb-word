2. Data Collection and Usage

The Data You Provide to Us

We collect your information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use the app, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our apps, when you participate in activities on the app, or send us feedback. The information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the app. User information we collect may include: user name, email address.

Automatic Information Collection

  • App Information: App version code, premium status, feedback type.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version (API level), language/locale.
  • Usage Information: Install timestamp or days since install, feedback timestamp, notification permission status.
  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics and Firebase to collect aggregated usage data to improve app stability and features.

Application Permission Requirements

  • STORAGE PERMISSION: For accessing and managing files (PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
  • ALL FILES ACCESS PERMISSION: For reading documents across folders.
  • FOREGROUND SERVICE: For background operations such as notifications or scanning services.
  • EXACT ALARM & SCHEDULE ALARM: For features like reminders and auto-checking services.
  • NOTIFICATION: For reminders, updates, and interactive alerts.
  • WAKE LOCK: To keep the device awake during certain actions.
  • ACCESS NETWORK STATE & INTERNET: For fetching online content and serving ads.
  • RECEIVE BOOT COMPLETED: To restart background services when the device is rebooted.
  • AD_ID: Used for advertising and analytics purposes.
  • CAMERA: For taking pictures and inserting to documents.
  • NOTIFICATION PERMISSION: Used to send reminders and updates. We may check whether notification permission is granted to enhance user experience.

Data Usage

The information collected is used to:

  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Resolve technical problems and respond to feedback.
  • Analyze app usage trends (e.g., number of days since install).
We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Email Feedback

If you choose to contact us via email through the app's feedback option, certain information may be automatically included in the email body to help us resolve your issue faster. This information may include: app version code, premium status, feedback type, device model, OS version (API level), language/locale, install timestamp, notification permission status, and feedback timestamp.

This information is visible to you in the email draft before you send it, and you may edit or remove it at your discretion. Sending the email is entirely voluntary and under your control. The data is only transmitted to us if you choose to send the email.
